Adrian M. Ionescu

EPFL

Scalable Semiconducting Qubits

In this presentation, we will outline the long-term vision of the Swiss NCCR SPIN and present some of our research activities related to scalable, semiconductor-based qubits. We will highlight recent advancements in integrating thin cobalt (Co) nanomagnets using a front-end-of-line compatible process for electron dipole spin resonance (EDSR) manipulation in Si spin qubits. Our focus will be on a case study involving the design, fabrication, and electrical characterization of compact spin qubit architectures with Co nanomagnets on Fully Depleted Silicon-on-Insulator (FDSOI), with magnet-to-quantum dot pitches ranging from 100 nm down to 10 nm. Additionally, we will discuss the competing FinFET qubit technology platform and its associated figures of merit.
