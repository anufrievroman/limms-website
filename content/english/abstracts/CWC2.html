October 10-11, 2024 - Tokyo, Japan

Prof. Chih-Wei Chang

Center for Condensed Matter Sciences, National Taiwan University, Taipei, Taiwan

NTU-CNRS collaboration project

National Taiwan University (NTU) is excited to embark on collaborative research with CNRS-LIMMS, with NTU's Center for Condensed Matter Sciences (CCMS) serving as the host institute. This collaboration involves 14 PIs from various departments, including Physics, Mechanical Engineering, Material Science and Engineering, and Chemical Engineering, alongside PIs from CCMS. We wish to work with our partners on research themes covering Quantum Materials and Technology, Energy and Carbon Neutrality, and Advanced Microscopy/Spectroscopy. In the long run, we aim to build strategic alliance labs with our international partners.
