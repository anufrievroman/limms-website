Europe/Asia Workshop on Energy, Bioengineering and Quantum & Molecular technologies
(EURALIMMS 2024)

October 10-11, 2024 - Tokyo, Japan

Organized at the Institute of Industrial Science, Komaba campus, the University of Tokyo − LIMMS, Convention Hall AN 2nd floor. This workshop is the kick-off of the EURALIMMS International Research Network between CNRS (France), Tokyo University (Japan), NTU (Ta√Øwan), Helmoltz (Germany), NUS (Singapore), EPFL (Switzerland), SNU (South Korea), Twente University (The Netherlands).
